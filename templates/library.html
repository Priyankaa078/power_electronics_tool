Component Library

Browse our collection of power electronics components and converter topologies.

Resistor

A passive component that limits current flow.

Parameters:
  • Resistance (ohms)
  • Power Rating (watts)
Capacitor

Stores energy in an electric field.

Parameters:
  • Capacitance (farads)
  • Voltage Rating
  • ESR (Equivalent Series Resistance)
Inductor

Stores energy in a magnetic field.

Parameters:
  • Inductance (henries)
  • Current Rating
  • DC Resistance
Diode

Allows current to flow in one direction only.

Parameters:
  • Forward Voltage
  • Reverse Current
  • Current Rating
MOSFET

Voltage-controlled switch for power electronics.

Parameters:
  • Rds(on) - On-state Resistance
  • Threshold Voltage
  • Current Rating
IGBT

Combines MOSFET and BJT characteristics for high power applications.

Parameters:
  • Vce(sat) - Saturation Voltage
  • Threshold Voltage
  • Current Rating
DC Voltage Source

Provides constant voltage.

Parameters:
  • Voltage (V)
  • Maximum Current
PWM Source

Generates pulse-width modulated signal for switch control.

Parameters:
  • Amplitude
  • Frequency
  • Duty Cycle
Buck Converter

Step-down DC-DC converter.

Components:
  • Switch (MOSFET/IGBT)
  • Diode
  • Inductor
  • Capacitor
  • Load
Boost Converter

Step-up DC-DC converter.

Components:
  • Switch (MOSFET/IGBT)
  • Diode
  • Inductor
  • Capacitor
  • Load
Buck-Boost Converter

Can step voltage up or down.

Components:
  • Switch (MOSFET/IGBT)
  • Diode
  • Inductor
  • Capacitor
  • Load
